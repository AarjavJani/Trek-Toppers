Overview

Mount Girnar is older than the Himalayas and the Jain temples upon it are amongst the most ancient in the country. The base of the mountain, known as Girnar Taleti, is about 4 km east of the center of Junagadh. Girnar – a group of mountains in the Junagadh district of Gujarat are a haven for thrill seekers and religious devotees alike due to their ability to be both – a hub for religious activity and also a retreat for trekkers, because of the hills that lead up to splendid views.

Itinerary

  • 10:00 AM : Arrival at Junagadh, greet and meet at Bhavnath Taleti.
  • 12:00 PM : After Damo Kund bathing refreshment, take lunch at hotel then depart for most awaited trek from Bhavnath Taleti – GIRNAR TREK.
  • 04:00 PM : Reach at Hanumandhara, and take break for some time. Spend some time, see mesmerising beauty around and enjoy Maggie in lap of lush green mountains. *We can reach at the top of the highest mountain of Gujarat if weather and time permits.
  • 06:00 PM: After the summit of highest peak of Gujarat, hike back to Bhavnath Taleti.
  • 09:00 PM : Later you can depart carrying beautiful memories.
